Flowchart to find whether a number is prime or composite

1.Start
2.Accept a positive integer{number greater than 2}
3.Initalize counter with value 2
4.Divide the number with 2
5.If the remainder is 0 display that positive number is composite and stop the algorithm.
6.Else increment the 'counter' and repeat from step 4.untill the 'counter' value is less than the 'n'
7.After completion of all the steps display that a number 'n' is prime
8.stop
